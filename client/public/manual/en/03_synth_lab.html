Concept

The Synth Lab allows you to compose basslines and melodies. It features two independent synthesis engines:

  • Lead : For main melodies.
  • Bass : For low frequencies.

The interface now features a distinctive Cyberpunk Green theme to differentiate it from the Juicy Box (Orange).

AI Composer ‚ú®

Running out of inspiration? Click the "Generate (AI)" button in the toolbar.

  • Artificial Intelligence will analyze your style and the track's global key.
  • It will automatically generate a coherent melody or bassline.
  • You can accept the proposal or request a new variation.

Piano Roll

Melodies are written on a Piano Roll.

  • Add Note : Click on the grid.
  • Change Pitch : Drag note up/down.
  • Duration : Stretch the right edge of the note to extend it.

Sound Design

The right side panel gives you access to synth parameters:

  • Oscillator : Waveform (Sine, Square, Sawtooth, Triangle).
  • Envelope (ADSR) : Attack, Decay, Sustain, Release.
  • Filter : Cutoff and Resonance to sculpt the sound's brightness.
