From Loop to Full Song

By default, Boumb'App plays active patterns in a loop (Loop Mode). The Skyline is the arrangement view that allows you to structure a full song over time.

Using the Timeline

  • Clips : Blocks containing your drum or melody patterns.
  • Arrangement : Place clips on the horizontal timeline to define when they play (Intro, Verse, Chorus...).
  • Navigation : Use the scrollbar or wheel to zoom in/out of your track.

"Matrix" Debug Panel

An advanced monitoring panel is displayed over the Skyline:

  • Visual : "Digital Rain" (Matrix) style with CRT scanlines.
  • Info : Displays elapsed time, current step, CPU usage, and active patterns.
  • Status : Clearly indicates if the audio engine is PLAYING or READY.
