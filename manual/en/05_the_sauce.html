Mixing Console

Each track (Kick, Snare, Lead...) has its own Channel Strip to sculpt the final sound.

  • Fader : Controls volume.
  • VU Meter : Visualize sound level in real-time (Green/Yellow/Red).
  • Mute (M) / Solo (S) : Silence or isolate tracks.
  • Master : The red fader on the right controls overall output volume, with an integrated Spectrum Analyzer.

Effects (FX)

The effects panel has been redesigned with a modern frosted glass interface (Glassmorphism). New effects available:

  • Reverb : Space and depth.
  • Delay : Rhythmic echoes.
  • BitCrusher : Digital lo-fi degradation (8-bit effect).
  • AutoFilter : Modulated automatic filter (Wah-wah, sweeps).
  • Distortion : Aggressive saturation.

Click the "Plus" (+) on a track to add an effect to the chain.
