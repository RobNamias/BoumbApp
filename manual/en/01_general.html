The Interface (TopBar)

The top bar is your main command center. It is divided into several zones:

  • Transport (Left) :
    • Play Space, Pause, Stop.
    • BPM : Project Tempo.
    • Measure Indicator : Displays current position (Bar:Beat:Step).
  • Global Key :

    Define the musical key of your track (e.g., C Major). All AI generators and keyboards will automatically tune to this scale.

  • Project & Account (Right) :
    • Cloud Save : Floppy disk button to save your work.
    • Profile : Click on your avatar to access the Unified Dashboard.

Unified Dashboard

Accessible via your profile icon, this is your personal space:

  • Producer Identity : Edit your stage name and avatar.
  • Cloud Studio : Find all your saved projects. Load or delete them in one click.
