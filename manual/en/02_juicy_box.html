Overview

The Juicy Box is a Step Sequencer designed for beatmaking.

  • 32-Step Grid : 2 bars of 16 steps for complex rhythms.
  • 8 Tracks Maximum : A concise selection to keep focus.

Sample Management (Drag & Drop)

Use the side panel (Browser) to find your sounds:

  • Preview : Click a sample in the list to listen before loading.
  • Drag & Drop :
    • Drag a sample onto an existing track to replace it.
    • Drag a sample onto an empty area to create a new track.

Pattern Editing

To create a beat, simply click on the "Steps" (blocks) of the grid.

Advanced Functions (Right Click / Shift)

Each step has hidden properties to humanize your beat:

  • Velocity (Volume) : Hold click and drag up/down to change a note's volume.
  • Fill (Probability) : Trigger a note randomly (e.g., 50% chance) to create variations.
